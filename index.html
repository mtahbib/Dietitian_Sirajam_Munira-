    Find Out More About Shirajam Munira

    Shirajam Munira a young dietitian planning meal, food, and nutrition programs, and supervising the preparation and serving of meals. Practicing to prevent and treat illnesses by promoting healthy eating habits and recommending dietary. She graduated & has a post-graduated in Food and Nutrition from the Islamic University, Bangladesh.

    She is currently a consultant nutritionist at CARe Medical College and Hospital Ltd. located at College Gate, Dhaka, and Ibn Sina Medical College and Hospital in Kalyanpur, Dhaka. Before her current role, she served as a senior dietitian at Justfit Healthcare (BD) LTD and held the position of dietitian at Vibes Healthcare Limited and VLCC Healthcare Limited, both situated in Dhaka.

    Major Interest in:

    • Sports Nutrition
